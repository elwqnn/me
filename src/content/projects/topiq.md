A lightweight TCP-based publish/subscribe message broker built in Rust. 6-crate workspace with a custom wire protocol, trie-based subject routing with wildcards, queue groups for load balancing, and request/reply RPC. Embeddable as a library or deployable as a standalone server. Published on crates.io.
